PMA SAFI HR Institute



PMA SAFI HR INSTITUTE, situated at Vazhayoor, near Calicut in Kerala, is a human resources development arm of Social Advancement Foundation of India (SAFI), a non-profit educational charity trust founded by an eminent group of Educationalists, Philanthropists and Social Businessmen.

The institute has been approved by AICTE and offers different leadership and management educational programs as well as training, research, and consultancy activities for empowering citizens in general, and backward/disadvantaged classes in particular, by fostering in them leadership capabilities integrated with strong values and social responsibility.

The Institute, however, does not compete with other Institutes but collaborates with all stakeholders to craft the most appropriate and rigorous programs suited for different target groups.


Leadership Development - AICTE Approved courses.

At the HR Institute, we believe that leadership effectiveness or organizational effectiveness does not come from leadership skills alone. Besides the managerial and functional skills, the leaders need to be driven by invisible forces in them in the form of ethical and human values including a sense of social responsibility, which is our motto guiding all the programs. The following are our AICTE approved Programs:

Post Graduate Diploma Program in Management

2- year program on managerial and ethical leadership skills
Post Graduate Certificate (PGCM) in Social Enterprises Management

1-year program on managing social enterprises ensuring social impact and sustainability
Post Graduate Certificate (PGCM) in Public Policy & Management
